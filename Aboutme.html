Background

I am 21 years of age, I'll be 22 this October. I started coaching basketball right after highschool. When I started I was only a helper/assisstant coach. A year later I was given the oppertunity to be the head coach for JV, which made me one of the youngest coaches in the city to coach a team. This fall will be two years of coaching highschool basketball at the Junior Varisty level at Saint Anthony High School. One thing I love about coaching is teaching a kid that has no experince in basketball, begin to show progress in practice and in game. Winning games truly isn't my main concern but it defintely feels great knowing your boys are playing good defense and running great offensive sets.
